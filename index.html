Sustainable Living

How can we make living more sustainable?

Here are 4 ways that you can instantly live more sustainable.

1. Think Before You Shop.

Every product that we buy leaves an enviromental footprint. From the material we use to create it during manufacturing, tot he packing that ends up in landfill and incinerators.

Only buy what you need, and look for products made from lower-impact materials with minimal packaging and shipping.

2. Be water wise.

You can conserve water by taking shorter showers, fixing leaky toilets, and choosing low-flow and low-water appliance options.

Drive less, drive green.

Fossil-fueled transportation emissions create greenhouse gases, smog, soot and other harmful air pollution.

When you can, walk, bike, carpool or use public transport. This can help drastically reduce your carbon footprint.
